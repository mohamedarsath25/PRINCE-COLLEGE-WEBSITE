About Us

A Legacy of Engineering Excellence

Founded in 2009, PDKVCET stands as a premier institution in Chennai, committed to academic rigor, innovative research, and holistic student development. Affiliated to Anna University and approved by AICTE, the college has earned NAAC A+ accreditation with a commendable score of 3.4 out of 4.

Admission Process

UG:Via TNEA counseling based on +2 marks. Eligibility: 45–50% in 10+2.
PG:Via GATE / TANCET scores. Management quota also available.
Lateral:Direct 2nd year entry for diploma holders in relevant branches.
